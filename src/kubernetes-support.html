How we work

The flanksource model works by extending your team with a mix of principal and Senior Site Reliability engineers who are all CKA certified, with both development and production operations experience.

Chat First - We join your chat-rooms (Slack or MS Teams)
Joint Standups - We join your standups to keep up-to-date about your environment
Your Issue Tracking - Assign tasks directly to your extending team directly in the issue tracker of your choice (Jira, ClickUp, etc)
Two-Tier Oncall - You get a dedicated email address linked to our OpsGenie on-call rotation, enabling rapid reponse. Optionally we join your on-call system.
